Getting There

By Air: Biju Patnaik International Airport (BBI) is well-connected to Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Kolkata, and Chennai — approximately 15 min from all venues.

By Rail: Bhubaneswar Railway Station is a major junction on the East Coast line, with direct trains from most cities — about 10 min from all venues.

Weather Forecast

Bhubaneswar in mid-June is warm and humid with temperatures around 34–38°C during the day and 26–28°C at night. Light cotton and breathable fabrics are recommended. Brief evening showers are possible — carry a light umbrella just in case.
